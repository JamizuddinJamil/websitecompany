Welcome to Company Name, your trusted destination for all your agricultural needs. As the leading distributor of Massey Ferguson tractors in Malaysia and KINTA tractors in Southeast Asia, we take pride in providing high-quality solutions to our valued customers.

Our Story

Building Trust Since 1993

With deep roots in the tractor industry, Company Name has earned a solid reputation throughout Malaysia. We offer a comprehensive range of agricultural equipment, implements, spare parts, and technical services, carefully tailored to meet your diverse requirements.

At Company Name, we uphold professionalism as a cornerstone of our approach. Our experienced team, with over 30 years of industry expertise, is dedicated to continually improving every aspect of our business.

By staying focused on our core values, we strive to deliver exceptional products and services that exceed your expectations.
